1️⃣ Problem Identification

Indian farmers struggle with uncertain crop yields, pest invasions, and inconsistent soil fertility. CropAI was developed to bridge this gap using Generative AI and Satellite Data Analytics — empowering data-driven, personalized farming decisions.

2️⃣ Data Collection & Preprocessing

  • Soil Data: pH, NPK, and organic carbon levels gathered from regional datasets and surveys.
  • Weather Data: Live feeds from OpenWeather and NASA POWER APIs.
  • Satellite Data: NDVI & NDWI indices from Sentinel-2 for vegetation health tracking.
  • Pest & Fertilizer Data: Verified datasets from ICAR and FAO sources.

3️⃣ Model Development & AI Pipelines

  • Gemini + RAG Pipeline: Conversational intelligence retrieving contextual agricultural data.
  • YOLOv11 Disease Detection: Identifies 20+ leaf diseases with 92% accuracy.
  • LLM Fertilizer & Financial Advisors: Generate fertilizer plans, ROI forecasts, and recommendations.
  • Dynamic Crop Suggestor: Combines soil, pH, and rainfall to recommend best crops.

4️⃣ System Architecture

CropAI is built on a 3-tier modular architecture ensuring scalability and integration efficiency:

  • Frontend: React dashboard with modular AI advisors.
  • Backend: Flask REST APIs connecting Gemini, RAG, and ML modules.
  • Database: PostgreSQL for storing user and field analytics.
  • AI Pipelines: RAG + Gemini for chat, YOLOv11 for vision tasks.

5️⃣ Crop Room — Core Innovation

The Crop Room is the centerpiece of CropAI — a personalized AI workspace per farmer, dynamically adapting to soil and environmental conditions.

  • 🌾 Crop Suggestor: Provides region-specific recommendations.
  • 🧪 Fertilizer Advisor: Suggests precise dosage and timing.
  • 🐛 Pest Advisor: Predicts and mitigates pest outbreaks.
  • 💰 Financial Advisor: Calculates ROI and profitability insights.
  • 🧠 Doubt Solver: Handles natural queries via LLM.

6️⃣ Evaluation & Results

  • YOLOv11 Model Accuracy: 92% (Top-1) and 96% (Top-5)
  • RAG-Gemini Chat Accuracy: 89% contextual precision
  • Response Time: Average 2.4 seconds per query

7️⃣ Future Enhancements

  • Integration with IoT sensors for live soil data.
  • Time-series satellite data for predictive disease analytics.
  • Voice chatbot with multilingual capabilities.
  • Drone-based NDVI imaging for crop health mapping.
  • Blockchain-enabled traceability for supply-chain transparency.
