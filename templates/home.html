Key Modules of CropAI

Crop Room Dashboard

The Crop Room Dashboard acts as the central hub for farmers, combining all real-time field data in one intelligent interface. It continuously monitors soil health, crop growth stages, and irrigation status using satellite indices such as NDVI and NDWI. Through AI-powered insights, it visualizes patterns of vegetation health and detects anomalies early, empowering farmers to make precise and data-driven decisions on crop scheduling, fertilization, and pest management.

Crop & Fertilizer Advisor

The Crop & Fertilizer Advisor intelligently analyzes soil chemistry, pH levels, nutrient ratios, and local weather forecasts to recommend the most profitable and sustainable crop choices for the season. It also generates fertilizer plans customized to growth stages, combining organic and chemical recommendations for optimal yield. Leveraging RAG and Gemini AI, the system offers step-by-step, adaptive guidance — helping farmers fine-tune input management while maintaining ecological balance.

Pest & Disease Diagnosis

The Pest & Disease Diagnosis module employs computer vision and deep learning models (YOLOv11 + Gemini) to detect early symptoms of plant stress, leaf blight, and pest infestations from uploaded or live-captured images. It not only identifies the disease but also explains probable causes, affected regions, and recommended treatments — both organic and chemical. Farmers can interact through natural-language queries to receive real-time prevention strategies, ensuring quick recovery and minimized losses.

Financial Analytics

The Financial Analytics module transforms traditional farming into a data-driven business. It calculates the complete cost-to-profit chain — including seed expenditure, fertilizer cost, water usage, labor input, and expected market value. Integration with PMFBY (crop insurance), Kisan Credit Card schemes, and agri-fintech APIs allows real-time subsidy tracking and credit evaluation. By combining yield predictions with historical financial trends, the system provides farmers with ROI projections and practical recommendations for financial resilience.

About CropAI

CropAI bridges the gap between technology and agriculture by empowering farmers with Generative AI-driven analytics. Using a fusion of soil databases, satellite imagery, and predictive ML, CropAI helps farmers make better crop, fertilizer, and financial decisions.
